Caprese Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 pound fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Assemble the Salad:
    • Alternate slices of tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and whole basil leaves on a serving platter.
  2. Drizzle with Olive Oil:
    • Drizzle extra-virgin olive oil over the salad.
    • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Balsamic Glaze (Optional):
    • For extra flavor, drizzle with balsamic glaze if desired.

Enjoy this refreshing Caprese Salad as a side or a light appetizer!
